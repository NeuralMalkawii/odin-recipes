Lasagna

Home

Classic lasagna recipe, sheets of pasta are layered with a cheesy filling, a rich meaty tomato sauce, and more cheese and then baked until bubbly and browned.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach (variation)

How to Make Lasagna

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
